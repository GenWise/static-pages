Why this work matters

Talent is spread evenly. Opportunity is not.

In every classroom there is a child who asks one more question than the rest, who wants to know how and why, who lights up at a hard problem. Most never get a mentor, a harder challenge, or someone who notices in time. Not because of ability. Because the door was never there.

Godrej Tomorrow Makers, an initiative of the Godrej Foundation, exists to widen that door. GenWise runs its Std 6 to 7 STEM nurturing track, bringing a decade of mentoring and pedagogy to students who would otherwise never reach us. The programme is free, fully online, and built around curiosity rather than exam drilling.
